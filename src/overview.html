FoodMood is an application and service for analyzing the correlation between foods eaten and moods experienced on a daily basis. Users log the foods they eat and the moods they experience each day. The analytics views show a log of foods and moods recorded, a line chart showing the mood levels over time, and a scatterplot that shows the correlation between the number of foods eaten in a food group and the moods experienced.

This is group 7's project for IST 412 Fall 2017.
